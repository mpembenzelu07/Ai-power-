Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about Mzuri Stores

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, Mastercard, M-Pesa Tanzania, Airtel Money, bank transfers, and cash on delivery for local orders in Dar es Salaam.

How does the AI product recommender work?

Our AI analyzes your browsing behavior, purchase history, and similar customer profiles to suggest products you're most likely to be interested in. It learns and improves over time.

Can I verify product certificates on blockchain?

Yes! We use blockchain technology to verify product certificates. You can check any certificate's authenticity using our verification tool on the Blockchain page.

Do you ship outside Tanzania?

Yes, we ship to Kenya, Uganda, Rwanda, and other East African countries. International shipping is available for bulk orders.

How do I track my shipment?

Use our real-time shipment tracker on the Shipping page. Enter your tracking number or use voice commands with our AI assistant.

What AI tools are available for business customers?

Business customers get access to AI documentation wizard, bulk order simulator, lead qualifier, customer journey predictor, and smart compliance checker. Visit the AI Tools page for details.
